Privacy Policy

YT Shield – Ad Blocker & Auto-Refresh  ·  Last updated: June 2025

TL;DR: YT Shield collects no data whatsoever. Nothing leaves your browser.

What data we collect

None. YT Shield does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data, browsing data, or usage data.

What the extension does locally

YT Shield operates entirely within your browser. It does the following, all locally:

  • Injects CSS and JavaScript into YouTube pages to remove ad elements and end cards
  • Blocks network requests to known ad-serving domains using Chrome's declarativeNetRequest API
  • Monitors the YouTube video player for errors and triggers a page refresh if one is detected
  • Saves your feature toggle preferences (on/off) using Chrome's storage.sync API — this is stored in your browser and synced across your own Chrome profile only

Data we do NOT collect

  • No browsing history
  • No YouTube watch history or video data
  • No personal identifiers (name, email, IP address)
  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No crash reports sent to any server

Third-party services

YT Shield has no backend server, no analytics service, and no third-party integrations of any kind. The extension communicates with no external server.

Permissions explained

The permissions requested by YT Shield are the minimum required for its features to work:

  • tabs — to detect YouTube page navigation and reset the auto-refresh counter
  • scripting — to inject the ad-removal content script into YouTube pages
  • storage — to save your feature toggle preferences locally
  • declarativeNetRequest — to block ad network requests before they load

Children's privacy

YT Shield does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.
